**Salacious News** is a satirical news website dedicated to one thing: gossip, served with a wink.

We cover the stories that other outlets are too dignified to touch — celebrity drama, political absurdity, business scandals, and all the delicious dirt in between. Every article on this site is a work of satire and parody. Our headlines are designed to entertain, not inform, and nothing published here should be taken as factual reporting.

**We are not a real news organization.** We are storytellers, humorists, and professional rumor-mongers. When we link to original news sources, it is to provide context for the parody — not to claim those outlets endorse our spin.

**Why "Salacious"?** Because respectable news is boring. We believe the world is more interesting when viewed through a lens of irreverence, and we think you deserve to laugh at the headlines as much as you're expected to cry about them.

**Our Editorial Standard**

- Every article is clearly presented as satire.
- We link to original source material where applicable.
- We do not fabricate quotes or make genuine defamatory claims.
- Our legal disclaimer appears on every page.
