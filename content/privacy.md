This privacy policy applies to the website Salacious News (salacious.news).

**Data Collection**

We do not collect any personal information from visitors to our website. We use cookies and similar technologies solely to display advertisements and analyze site traffic.

**Cookies**

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer by websites you visit. They are used to remember preferences, track browsing activity, and serve relevant advertisements.

We use the following services that may place cookies on your device:

- **Google Analytics** — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and anonymous and is not used to identify you personally. You can opt out by installing the [Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

- **Adsterra** — We use Adsterra to display advertisements on our site. Adsterra and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests. For more information, see [Adsterra's Privacy Policy](https://adsterra.com/privacy-policy/).

**Third-Party Advertising**

Ads appearing on Salacious News may be delivered by Adsterra and its partners. These third parties may use information about your visits to this and other websites to provide targeted advertisements. We do not control the cookies or tracking technologies used by these partners.

**Your Rights**

You have the right to access any personal information we hold about you and to request its deletion. Since we do not collect personal information directly, most requests should be directed to the third-party services listed above.

To contact us directly, reach us at info@salacious.news.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@salacious.news.
